Nice to Meet You!

My name is Jorge Hernandez, and I am a 22-year-old recent graduate of Stephen F. Austin State University
with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I am looking to begin my career as a Software Developer
and aim to showcase my skills through this website.

I have known that I wanted to major in Computer Science ever since I was in middle school. It first started
as a desire to develop video games. I began researching online how to become a game developer and learned
that Computer Science was the perfect field that would allow me to pursue that career, however, it would
also open the door to other career paths if game development didn't work out.

After taking my introductory Computer Science course, I fell in love with the ability to solve problems
efficiently. Everywhere I looked, I saw a way to apply what I learned in my class and would generate idea
after idea for software such as apps and websites that would make my day-to-day life easier. It took one
attempt at making an app before I quickly realized just how much I had to learn before I would be able
to make my ideas come to life.

Now I sit at the culmination of my four years of hard work and can confidently say that I am able to make
efficient, responsive, and practical software. I started with the creation of this website to showcase my web
development skills in HTML and CSS. Then, I began working on an app which I named “OnTrack” with the purpose
of facilitating my financial tracking methods. After successfully completing these two projects, I now turn
to the corporate world to see who I can develop software for that will help more lives than just my own.
